Formal & Analytical – Structured, objective, and detail-oriented.
Casual & Conversational – Feels like a friend’s opinion.
Satirical/Ironic – Uses exaggerated praise or mockery.
Poetic/Lyrical – Employs vivid metaphors and flowery language.
Ranting – Long, emotional, and unfiltered criticism.
Concise & Direct – Short, to-the-point verdicts (e.g., "Waste of time.").
Humorous/Witty – Pokes fun at the film while reviewing it.
Academic/Theoretical – References film theory or critical essays.
Fanboy/Gushing – Overwhelmingly positive with little critique.
Clickbait-Style – Uses hyperbolic language for engagement.

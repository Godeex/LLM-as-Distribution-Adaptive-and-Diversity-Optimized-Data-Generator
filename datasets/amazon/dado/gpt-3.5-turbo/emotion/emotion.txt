Enthusiastic  – Unfiltered hype, often calling the product 'perfect' or 'game-changing.'
Disappointed / Regretful – A letdown review, usually starting with high expectations that weren’t met.
Neutral / Balanced – Fair and objective, listing both strengths and weaknesses calmly.
Frustrated / Angry – Venting about flaws, bad quality, or feeling scammed—sometimes aggressively.
Skeptical / Cautiously Optimistic – Tempered praise with reservations, often waiting to see long-term performance.

Before & After Comparison - Show how the product improved (or ruined) the user’s old routine.
Expectation vs. Reality - Exposes whether the product lives up to its marketing claims.
Use-Case Specific - Tailors the review to a specific need.
Long-Term Testing - Reveals durability and hidden flaws after extended use.
Price vs. Value- Answer: ‘Is this worth the money, or should you spend more/less?’
Satirical Absurdity - Use exaggerated humor to mock or praise the product.
